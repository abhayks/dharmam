# The Bhagavad Gita: A Chapter-wise Summary

## Chapters

### Chapter 1: The Yoga of Arjuna

> The Yoga of Arjuna

---

### Chapter 2: Sankhya Yoga (The Yoga of Knowledge)

> An analytical study of the nature of the soul and the path of selfless action.

---

### Chapter 3: Karma Yoga (The Yoga of Action)

> The path of selfless action, performed as a duty without attachment to results.

---

### Chapter 4: Jnana Karma Sannyasa Yoga (The Yoga of Knowledge)

> Understanding divine knowledge, the nature of action, and the wisdom of renunciation in action.
