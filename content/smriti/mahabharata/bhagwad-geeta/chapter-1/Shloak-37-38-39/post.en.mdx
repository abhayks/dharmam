# The Domino Effect of War

Arjuna connects the dots between war and social collapse.
1.  **Verses 37-38:** The Kauravas are blinded by greed, but we (the Pandavas) should know better than to kill our kin.
2.  **Verse 39:** Why? Because killing the elders destroys **Kula-dharma** (Family Traditions). Elders are the keepers of wisdom and ritual. If they die prematurely, the knowledge is not passed down, and the remaining family falls into **Adharma** (Chaos/Impiety).

## Description
**1.37 – 1.39**

"Their eyes are shut by greed and pride,<br />
They cannot see the turning tide.<br />
They see no sin in family slaughter,<br />
Pouring blood like running water.

But we, O Krishna, still can see,<br />
The fruit of this catastrophe.<br />
If a madman holds a burning brand,<br />
Should not the sane man stay his hand?

For when the elders fall and die,<br />
Who will teach the young, and why?<br />
The ancient rites, the sacred way,<br />
Will vanish in a single day.

When wisdom’s chain is snapped in two,<br />
Chaos rushes in on cue.<br />
Without the guide of Dharma’s light,<br />
The family sinks into the night."
