## Description
**4.31**

Those who partake of sacrifice's nectar, pure and bright,<br />
Attain eternal Brahman, in everlasting light.<br />
No joy awaits the sacrificeless here, O best of Kuru's line,<br />
How then, the other world, for those who fail to shine?
