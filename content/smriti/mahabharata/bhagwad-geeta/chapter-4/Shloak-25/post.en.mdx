## Description
**4.25**

Some yogis offer sacrifice to gods alone,<br />
While others into Brahman's fire their offerings have thrown.<br />
By sacrifice to sacrifice, their sacred rites they ply,<br />
In unity with the Divine, their spirits soar on high.
