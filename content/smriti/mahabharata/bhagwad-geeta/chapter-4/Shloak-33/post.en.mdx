## Description
**4.33**

Better than wealth's offering, is wisdom's sacred flame,<br />
O Parantapa, know this truth, and glorify My name.<br />
For all actions culminate, in knowledge pure and bright,<br />
Ending the dark of ignorance, bringing eternal light.
