## Description
**3.41**

Therefore, O best of Bharatas, control the senses first,<br />
And slay this sinful destroyer, this hunger and this thirst.<br />
Which ruins knowledge and realization, keeping the soul accursed,<br />
Strike down this foe within you, before the bonds are burst.
