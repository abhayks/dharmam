## Description
**3.7**

But he who guides his senses with a mind controlled and clear,<br />
And acts without attachment, O Arjuna, him hold very dear.<br />
In Karma Yoga engaged fully, with organs disciplined,<br />
He excels the pretender, his soul by truth is pinned.
