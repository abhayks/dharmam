## Description
**3.37**

The Lord replied: It is Desire, it is Wrath, born of passion's fire,<br />
Insatiable and sinful, a seemingly endless mire.<br />
Know this as the enemy, the soul's most deadly dire,<br />
That burns the light of wisdom, and drags the spirit in pyre.
