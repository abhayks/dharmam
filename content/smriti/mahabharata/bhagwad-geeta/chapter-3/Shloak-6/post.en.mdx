## Description
**3.6**

Who checks the senses, yet in mind on objects dwells with greed,<br />
That deluded soul, a hypocrite, performs a deceptive deed.<br />
His outer form is calm and still, but inner storms do rage,<br />
A false pretender to the truth, upon the world's vast stage.
