## Description
**3.9**

Save actions done as sacrifice, this world by karma's bound,<br />
So work, O son of Kunti, free from ties, on sacred ground.<br />
Offer deeds to the Divine, as Yajna pure and bright,<br />
And walk the path of freedom, bathed in eternal light.
