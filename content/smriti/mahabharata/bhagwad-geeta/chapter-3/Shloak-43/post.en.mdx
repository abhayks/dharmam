## Description
**3.43**

Knowing the Self beyond the mind, establishing self by Self,<br />
Conquer this enemy, O mighty-armed, ignoring worldly pelf.<br />
Slay the form of desire, hard to conquer though it be,<br />
And stand in freedom absolute, eternally free.
