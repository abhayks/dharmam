## Description
**3.10**

With sacrifice, Prajapati created man of old,<br />
"By this," He said, "be fruitful, let your destinies unfold;<br />
Let this be your wish-fulfilling cow, more precious than pure gold,<br />
Through selfless service, prosper, and let your virtues hold."
