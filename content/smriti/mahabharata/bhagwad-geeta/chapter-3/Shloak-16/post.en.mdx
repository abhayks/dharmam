## Description
**3.16**

Who turns not with this cosmic wheel, set moving from the start,<br />
Lives sinfully and in vain, with senses ruling heart.<br />
Indulging in the fleeting joys, neglecting duty's call,<br />
He wastes his life in selfish play, and surely heads for fall.
