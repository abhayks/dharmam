## Description
**3.8**

Perform your duties, for action is greater than inaction's plight,<br />
Even your body's journey would cease, without labor's light.<br />
Prescribed works purify the mind, and keep the world in spin,<br />
While idleness destroys the soul, and lets the darkness in.
