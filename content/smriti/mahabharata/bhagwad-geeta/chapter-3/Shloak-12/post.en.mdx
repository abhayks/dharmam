## Description
**3.12**

Pleased by the sacrifice, the gods grants gifts of heart's desire,<br />
But he who takes without return, is a thief in mire.<br />
Enjoying bounty given freely, with no offering in return,<br />
Is stealing from the cosmic store, a lesson one must learn.
