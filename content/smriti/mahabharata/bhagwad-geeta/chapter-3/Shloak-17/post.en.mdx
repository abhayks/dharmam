## Description
**3.17**

But he who revels in the Self, and in the Self is pleased,<br />
Whose thirst for joy in outer things, has utterly been eased.<br />
For him no duty remains to do, no obligation binds,<br />
Since in the Self's eternal light, all fulfillment he finds.
