## Description
**3.1**

Arjuna spoke: O Janardana, if You deem wisdom’s path more grand,<br />
Than deeds of action, why compel this war, by Your command?<br />
O Keshava, this fearsome strife, why bid me take my stand?<br />
My heart is heavy, mind confused, by this dark, bloody land.
