## Description
**3.2**

Thy words confused, my mind perplexed, O Krishna, tell me true,<br />
One certain path to liberation, clear for me to pursue.<br />
Dispel the fog that clouds my sight, and let wisdom shy through,<br />
That I may find the highest good, and bid my doubts adieu.
