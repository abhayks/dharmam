## Description
**3.15**

Know actions spring from Brahma's Word, and Brahma from the Indestructible,<br />
Thus the all-pervading Truth, in sacrifice is visible.<br />
The Infinite in finite acts, is ever to be found,<br />
In every sacred rite performed, the Divine is all around.
