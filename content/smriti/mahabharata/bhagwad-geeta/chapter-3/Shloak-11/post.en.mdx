## Description
**3.11**

By this, nourish the gods above, and may they nourish you,<br />
In mutual love and service, find a welfare deep and true.<br />
A cycle of giving and receiving, sustaining life's grand play,<br />
Attain the highest good for all, in a harmonious way.
