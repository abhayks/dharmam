## Description
**3.18**

No gain has he from what is done, nor loss from what's undone,<br />
Dependence on the world of men, for him there is none.<br />
He stands alone, self-satisfied, beyond the need of aid,<br />
In freedom absolute and pure, his spirit unafraid.
