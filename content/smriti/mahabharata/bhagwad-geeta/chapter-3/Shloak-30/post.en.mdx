## Description
**3.30**

Surrendering all actions unto Me, the Lord Supreme,<br />
With mind intent on Self, awake from worldly dream.<br />
Free from desire and ego, let your courage beam,<br />
Fight on, O Arjuna, flow with the divine stream.
