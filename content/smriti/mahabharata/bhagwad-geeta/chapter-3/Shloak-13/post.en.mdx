## Description
**3.13**

The righteous eat the remnant food, from sacrifice released,<br />
From every sin they are absolved, their souls are hunger-eased.<br />
But those who cook for self alone, in selfishness they dine,<br />
They verily eat sin itself, ignoring laws divine.
