## Description
**3.21**

Whatever actions great men do, the common people trace,<br />
The standard that they set up, the whole world will embrace.<br />
If leaders walk the path of truth, the masses follow suit,<br />
But if they stray, the world goes wrong, and bears the bitter fruit.
