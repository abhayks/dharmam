## Description
**3.38**

As fire is veile'd by smoke, and mirrors by the dust,<br />
As embryos in the womb are hid, so wisdom by lust.<br />
This covering clouds the truth, in layers thick and crust,<br />
Blinding the soul's clear vision, with craving and mistrust.
