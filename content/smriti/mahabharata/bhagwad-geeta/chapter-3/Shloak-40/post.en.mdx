## Description
**3.40**

The senses, mind, and intellect, are seats where it resides,<br />
Through these it deludes the soul, and truth it cunningly hides.<br />
Veiling the light of wisdom, on waves of lust it rides,<br />
Confusing the embodied one, with false and shifting tides.
