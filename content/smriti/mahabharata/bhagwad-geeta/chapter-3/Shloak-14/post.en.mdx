## Description
**3.14**

From food all beings take their birth, from rain the corn does grow,<br />
From sacrifice the rain is born, from action sacrfices flow.<br />
The cycle turns on wheels of work, a cosmic interplay,<br />
Sustaining life in every form, from night until the day.
