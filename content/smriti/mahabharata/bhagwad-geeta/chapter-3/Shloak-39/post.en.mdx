## Description
**3.39**

Veiled is the wisdom of the wise, by this eternal foe,<br />
In form of desire insatiable, causing endless woe.<br />
Like fire it burns and never dies, however much you throw,<br />
O Kaunteya, know this enemy, and strike it a fatal blow.
